About Optium Fitness

Your Journey to Fitness Starts Here

Who We Are

Optium Fitness is a premier fitness gym that works for and with YOU to reach your fitness goals. At Optium Fitness, we never settle for less than what you deserve. We understand that it can be difficult to prioritize your health, which is why we offer our elite services to help YOU become the best version of yourself.

About the Owner

Josie Smith is the founder and owner of Optium Fitness. She started from the begininng and has been training for 6 years. Josie has always been passionate about fitness and in 2021, decided to begin training athletes like herself. She started with nutritional advice and workout plans, which gradually morphed into what is now Optium Fitness. Josie has successfully opened 3 other gyms in Ohio: Cleveland, Columbus, and Cincinnati.

Our Mission

At Optium Fitness, our mission is to guide individuals through their fitness journey. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced athlete, our team is here to help you stay consistent and continue to reach your goals .

Why Optium?

  • Personalized Training: Customized workout plans for all athletes at all ages and levels
  • State-of-the-Art Facility: Advanced equipment that gets the job done
  • Expert Coaching: Certified trainers who have worked with many famous athletes

Did You Know?
